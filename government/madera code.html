Madera Municipal Code (Current through Ordinance No. 833 C.S. adopted 3/19/08) The Madera Municipal Code is a compilation of all the regulatory, penal and administrative laws of general application of the City of Madera, codified pursuant to the authority contained in California Government Code Section 50022.2. The Municipal Code is updated, at minimum, semi-annually. To access the Madera Municipal Code, click here. For a listing of ordinances adopted after 3/19/08, click here. Copies containing the full text of listed ordinances may be viewed or purchased for a fee at the Office of the City Clerk, 205 West Fourth Street, Madera, California, 93637. For additional information, contact the Office of the City Clerk at (559) 661-5405.

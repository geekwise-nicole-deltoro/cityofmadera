Council Member District 5

Mayor Pro Tem Charles F. Rigby
Madera City Council District 5

District 5 Map

Charles Rigby is currently serving his first term in office as a member of the Madera City Council. Charles was first elected to the City Council in 2014 and will serve a four year term through 2018.

Charles is currently serving as Mayor Pro Tem and will serve as Mayor Pro Tem through December of 2016. The Mayor Pro Tem position is rotated annually amongst the Council Members serving.

Members of the City Council also serve as Board Members of the Successor Agency to the Former Madera Redevelopment Agency and as Commissioners of the Housing Authority of the City of Madera.

Charles has been a resident of Madera since 1984. He attended Madera schools and graduated from Madera High School in 1999. After high school he attended Occidental College and earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in 2003.

Charles is currently a pastor at Valley West Christian Center where he has served for the last 11 years. He also coaches boys basketball and golf for Madera Unified School District and has done so for the past 5 years.

Charles and his wife Clarissa have been married for five years. They have one son.

Professional Affiliations / Other Public Service

In addition to their regular duties as Council Members, Members of the City Council are appointed to serve on other boards as representatives of the City Council. They are also active in community organizations. Council Member Rigby is currently serving on or has served in the past on the following boards and programs:

San Joaquin River Conservancy (alternate board member)
Madera County Local Childcare and Development Planning Council Steering Committee (alternate board member)
Madera Ministerial Association
Madera Unified School District Adopt a School Program
Madera South High School Mentor Program
Madera Parks & Recreation Basketball Program
